# How Albi Sommelier Works

## What is Albi Sommelier?

Albi Sommelier is a smart wine recommendation system built into the app. It suggests ideal wines based on your preferences and needs through a series of targeted questions.

## What It Considers

- Your taste preferences (sweet, dry, tannic, fresh…)
- Food pairings for your meal
- The occasion and mood
- Your previous tasting history

## How to Use It

Open the app and find the **Sommelier** section. Answer the guided questions and Albi will suggest wines from the catalog that match your profile — including ones available as Smart Wine Bags.

## Wine Statistics

Track your personal wine journey via **Your Profile → Statistics**:

| Stat | What it tracks |
|------|---------------|
| Wines Consumed | Total Smart Wine Bags used |
| Label Variety | Number of different wines tried |
| Glass Count | Total glasses poured |

## Sustainability Note

Albicchiere Smart Bags reduce CO₂ emissions by **40%** compared to traditional glass bottles, through 80% less waste, 33% less volume in transport, and 20% energy savings in wine logistics.

## Can I Use Sparkling Wines?

Smart Bags are designed for **still wines** only. Sparkling wines (Prosecco, Champagne, Cava, etc.) are not compatible with the bag format due to carbonation pressure.
