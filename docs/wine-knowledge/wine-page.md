# Understanding the Wine Page

When you open a wine's detail page, you'll find:

## Basic Information
- Label image
- Cellar and vintage
- Wine name
- Remaining glasses

## Wine Characteristics
Scroll to discover:
- Colour and grape variety
- Region and appellation
- Alcohol content
- Recommended serving temperature
- Allergens

## Reviews and Ratings
- Community ratings and tasting notes
- Your own review (if you've tasted it)
- Verified reviews (marked with a red ✓ — tasted directly through an Albi dispenser)

## Food Pairings
Suggested food combinations for the wine, drawn from Albicchiere's pairing database.
