# Wine Moods and Philosophies

## Moods

Moods help you find a wine that matches how you feel. Each mood is associated with a style of wine:

| Mood | Wine Style |
|------|-----------|
| **Passionate** | Intense, expressive wines for emotional moments |
| **Meditative** | Wines for quiet, contemplative moments |
| **Carefree** | Light, easy-drinking wines for relaxed occasions |

Use mood filters in the Search section to discover wines that fit the moment.

## Wine Philosophies

Philosophies reflect the values behind how a wine is made:

| Philosophy | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| **Vegan** | Produced without animal-derived ingredients or processes |

More philosophies may be added as the Albicchiere wine database grows.
