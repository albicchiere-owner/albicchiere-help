# Manage Your Personal Data

## Edit Personal Information

1. Go to **Your Profile**
2. Select **"Settings and Privacy"**
3. Choose **"Data Management"**

You can update:
- First and last name
- Birthday
- Nationality
- Gender

All changes are saved automatically.

## Change Your Email Address

1. Go to **Your Profile → Settings and Privacy → Change Email**
2. Enter your current email address
3. Enter your new email address
4. Confirm and save

## Change App Settings

Go to **Profile → Settings and Privacy → Parameters Management** to change:
- App language
- Units of measurement
- Date format
- Time format

## Manage Shipping Addresses

Go to **Profile → Shipping Addresses** to:
- **Edit** an existing address (select it, modify, save)
- **Add** a new address (select "New Address", fill in fields, save)
