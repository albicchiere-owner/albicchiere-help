# Notifications and Privacy

## Manage Notifications

1. Go to **Your Profile**
2. Select **"Settings and Privacy"**
3. Choose **"Notification Management"**

Active notifications show a **ruby checkmark**. Inactive ones have no checkmark.

**How to check your notifications:**
Tap the **bell icon** in Explore. A golden bell means you have new unread notifications; a grey bell means all notifications have been read.

## Privacy Settings

1. Go to **Your Profile → Settings and Privacy**
2. Select **"Privacy"**

Here you can manage data sharing preferences and connected permissions.

## Enable GPS Location

Enable GPS to discover local wines and nearby wineries:

1. Go to **Profile → Settings and Privacy**
2. Find the **Location** setting
3. Enable GPS sharing

This allows Albi to provide personalised wine suggestions based on your area.
