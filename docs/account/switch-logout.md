# Switch Account and Logout

## Switch Between Accounts

1. Go to **Your Profile**
2. Select the **circle next to your name**
3. Choose the profile you want to switch to
4. Tap **X** to confirm

The app immediately updates to show the selected account.

## Log Out

1. Go to **Your Profile**
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Select **"Logout"**

## Permanently Exit an Account

If you want to completely remove an account from the app:

1. Go to **Your Profile → Logout section**
2. Select **"Exit Account"**
3. Confirm your choice

You can always log back in using your credentials or social login.
