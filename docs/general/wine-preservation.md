# How Long Does Wine Stay Preserved?

Every wine is different, so there's no single universal answer. However, as a general guideline:

> On average, a young wine in an Albicchiere Smart Bag stays perfectly preserved for **over 6 months** after the first opening.

For the best flavour, always follow the specific recommendations for the wine you've inserted — these are shown automatically in the app.

:::tip
The Smart Wine Bag's sealed, nitrogen-free environment is what makes such long preservation possible.
:::
