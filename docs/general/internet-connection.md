# Does Albi Need a Constant Internet Connection?

**No.** Albi will continue to preserve your wine even without an internet connection.

However, Albi relies on the internet to:

- Automatically identify your wine and retrieve its details
- Sync your cantina data with the app
- Receive software updates

We recommend keeping Albi connected for the best experience, but it's not required for basic operation.
