# How Does It Work?

Albi works automatically once set up.

1. **Insert** a Smart Wine Bag into the dispenser
2. **Auto-recognition** — Albi identifies the wine type, its preservation status, and characteristics
3. **Temperature control** — Albi heats or cools the wine based on the time you set to reach the recommended or preferred temperature
4. **Dispense** — pour a perfect glass whenever you want

Albi handles preservation automatically so you never have to worry about your open wine going bad.
