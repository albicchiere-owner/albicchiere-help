# What Is Albi?

The **Albicchiere Smart Wine Dispenser** is a cutting-edge IoT device that connects to your home ecosystem and gives you full control over your wine experience.

With Albi you can:

- 🍷 **Dispense wine** at the perfect temperature, every time
- 📱 **Control it remotely** via the dedicated app
- 🔊 **Connect to Alexa and Google Home** — ask for pairing suggestions or check how much wine is left
- ❄️ **Heat or cool** your wine automatically based on your preferences
- 🧠 **Auto-recognize** the inserted Smart Wine Bag and its contents

Albi is the most compact smart wine dispenser in its category. It will become your new best friend for romantic dinners or relaxing evenings.
