# Manage Your Cantina and Wishlist

## Cantina

Your cantina is your personal wine collection in the app.

**Gestures:**
- **Swipe left** → add to wishlist or manage parental controls
- **Swipe right** → delete wine from cantina
- **Tap a label** → view wine details

Use the **tabs** to quickly filter wines, or the **search bar** to find a specific bottle.

## Wishlist

Your wishlist lets you save wines you want to try.

**To add a wine:**
- From the wine page: tap the **heart icon**
- From Cantina or Search: **swipe** and select the heart

**To view your wishlist:**
- Tap the **heart icon** in Explore

If your wishlist is empty, start exploring wines in the catalog and save your favourites.
