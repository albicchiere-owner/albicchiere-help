# What to Do When Your Dispenser is Offline

## Network Checks

1. **Verify router status** — make sure your router is powered on and has an active internet connection
2. **Wi-Fi signal** — move Albi closer to the router if the signal is weak
3. **Restart Albi** — unplug for 30 seconds and plug back in
4. **Restart your router** — if other devices also have issues

## Still Offline?

If Albi remains offline after the above steps:
- Check that the Wi-Fi password hasn't changed
- Try performing a [factory reset](device-settings) and setting up Wi-Fi again
- Contact support with your device's [technical information](device-settings)

:::info
Remember: Albi will continue to preserve your wine and dispense locally even without internet. Only app control and wine recognition require connectivity.
:::
