# How to Add Wine Using Label Scan

Use your camera to quickly add wines to your cantina by scanning the bottle label.

## Steps

1. Tap the **Wine Glass icon**
2. Select **"Scan Label"**
3. Choose your method:
   - 📷 **Take a picture** — point your camera at the label
   - 🖼️ **Upload an image** — choose from your photo library

### After scanning

- **If successful:** Select the bottle size and tap **"Add to Cantina"**
- **If unsuccessful:** Try again with better lighting, or search for the wine manually
