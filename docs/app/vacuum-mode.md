# How to Use Vacuum Mode

Vacuum mode helps preserve your wine by removing air from the Smart Wine Bag, extending its freshness.

## Prerequisites

- Wine must be loaded in the dispenser
- Dispenser must be online

## Steps

1. Go to **Explore**
2. Select your dispenser card
3. Find the **Vacuum Mode** option in the dispenser controls
4. Activate and follow the on-screen instructions

:::tip
Use Vacuum Mode when you know you won't be drinking the wine for several days to maximise preservation.
:::
