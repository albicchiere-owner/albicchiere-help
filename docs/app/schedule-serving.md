# How to Schedule a Wine Serving

You can program Albi to have your perfect glass of wine ready exactly when you want it.

## Schedule a Serving

1. Navigate to the dispenser page
2. Scroll to the **scheduling section**
3. Tap **"Schedule"**
4. Choose your desired time
5. Confirm

## Modify or Cancel

To edit or cancel an existing scheduled serving:

1. Go to the dispenser page
2. Find the active schedule
3. Tap to **edit** (change time) or **cancel** (remove the schedule)
