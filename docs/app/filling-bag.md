# Filling Your Smart Wine Bag

Albicchiere sells refillable Smart Wine Bags. Follow these steps to fill them safely.

## Steps

1. **Position the bag** on the Pouring Assistant to keep it stable during filling
2. Open your wine bottle
3. **Pour slowly** into the bag opening to avoid spills and air bubbles
4. Once full, **seal the bag** with the cap following the two-step procedure
5. Store or insert immediately into Albi

:::tip
Fill the bag in a single session to minimise air exposure and maximise preservation time.
:::
