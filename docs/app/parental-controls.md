# How to Use Parental Controls

Parental controls let you restrict wine dispensing from the app.

## Two Types of Controls

### Dispenser Control
Prevents **all wine pouring** from the dispenser.

To manage:
1. Go to **Device Settings**
2. Select the **padlock icon**
3. Enable or disable from the modal

### Vintage Control
Restricts **specific wines or vintages**, applying to all bottles of the selected type.

To manage: swipe left on a wine in your cantina and select the parental control option.
