# How to Pour Wine from Your Dispenser

## Steps

With your dispenser on and a bag inserted:

1. Open the dispenser page in the app
2. Scroll down and select **"Pour"**
3. Choose your desired pour size
4. Confirm to start dispensing

## Temperature Alerts

The app may show two types of warnings before pouring:

- **"Not at Perfect Temperature"** — the wine is slightly off its ideal temperature. You can proceed or wait.
- **"We recommend waiting"** — the wine is significantly off temperature. For the best experience, let Albi continue conditioning.

You can always override and pour immediately if you prefer.
