# How to Convert Wine to Smart Wine Bag

Already have a wine in your cantina and want to move it into a Smart Wine Bag? Here's how.

## Steps

1. Go to your wine in the **cantina**
2. Select **"Convert into Smart Wine Bag"**
3. Place your phone near an empty Smart Wine Bag
4. Wait for the NFC tag scan to complete
5. Confirm the conversion

## Errors

| Error | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| NFC Tag Not Detected | Ensure tag and phone are close together |
| Invalid Tag | Make sure you're using an official Albicchiere Smart Bag |
