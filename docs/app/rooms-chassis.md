# Manage Rooms and Chassis

## What Are Rooms and Chassis?

- **Room** — a physical space where your dispenser is located (e.g. Kitchen, Living Room)
- **Chassis** — the physical frame/rack that holds one or more Albi dispensers

## View Rooms and Chassis

1. Go to **Your Profile**
2. Scroll down
3. Select **"Rooms and Chassis"**

## Create a New Room

For **Albi Home M+**:
1. Go to Device Settings → Edit (pencil icon)
2. Choose **"Create New Room"**
3. Enter a room name and save

## Rename a Room

1. Go to **Profile → Rooms and Chassis**
2. Stay in Rooms view
3. Select **Edit (pencil icon)** on the target room
4. Enter the new name and save

## Move Your Dispenser

1. Go to **Device Settings → Edit (pencil icon)**
2. Select **"Room"**
3. Choose the new room and confirm

## Delete a Room

A room can only be deleted if it has **no devices connected**.

1. Go to **Profile → Rooms and Chassis → Rooms tab**
2. Select the empty room
3. Tap **Delete** and confirm
