# How to Adjust Wine Serving Temperature

## Change Temperature Without Dispensing

1. Open the dispenser page
2. Select the **temperature control**
3. Scroll through available temperatures
4. Set to your preferred or suggested temperature
5. Confirm your selection

Albi will start heating or cooling to reach your target temperature.

## Cooling Modes

You can also choose how Albi manages cooling:

| Mode | Description | Best for |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Boost** | Maximum fan speed, fastest cooling | When you need wine quickly |
| **Eco** | Balanced performance, optimal energy use | Daily use (recommended default) |
| **Silent** | Minimal noise | Nighttime, quiet environments |

To change cooling mode: go to **Device Settings → Cooling Mode**.
