# What Does "Low Quantity" Mean?

When Albi shows **"Low Quantity"**, it's alerting you that the wine in your Smart Bag is running low.

## How it works

Albi tracks the quantity dispensed based on the pour size you select each time. When the estimated remaining amount drops below a threshold, this alert appears.

You might still have **0, ½ or 1 glass** remaining — the actual amount may vary slightly since Albi estimates quantity rather than measuring it precisely.

## What to do

- Dispense the remaining wine
- Replace the Smart Wine Bag with a new one
- Add the new bag to your cantina via the app
