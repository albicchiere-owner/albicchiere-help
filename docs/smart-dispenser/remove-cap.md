# How to Remove the Cap from the Smart Wine Bag

The Smart Wine Bag features a two-step closure system designed for safety and a perfect seal.

## Step 1 — Manual Preparation

Apply gentle pressure to the top of the cap to release the safety lock. You will feel a slight click.

## Step 2 — Full Removal

Rotate and pull the cap upward in one smooth motion to fully remove it.

:::caution
Do not force the cap. If it resists, repeat Step 1 before pulling.
:::
